# User Interviews

## Interviewed Profiles

- startup founders
- software engineers
- indie hackers

## Common Pain Points

### Lack of visibility
Teams do not know how much they spend on AI tools.

### Duplicate subscriptions
Multiple overlapping AI tools increase unnecessary spending.

### No optimization guidance
Most teams lack actionable recommendations.

## Key Insights

Users want:
- simple dashboards
- actionable savings recommendations
- automated reporting
- email summaries
